### 3.1 Digital Content and Product Management (Website and eBay)

- **Website and eBay product and listing updates**  
  We keep your **website** and **eBay** product listings accurate and up to date: new and used coffee machine descriptions, pricing, availability, images where provided, and key details. You no longer need to choose between serving customers and updating catalogues.
- **Content updates on your website**  
  Regular updates to pages, offers, and key information so your site stays current and supports the traffic you get from Google Ads and elsewhere.
- **Content planning**  
  A simple plan aligned to your product arrivals, promotions, and seasonal activity (e.g. peak coffee season) so updates are consistent and on brand.
- **Quality and brand alignment**  
  Listings and content produced in line with Coffee Machine Warehouse tone and guidelines. Approval workflow (e.g. per item or batch approval before we publish) will be agreed and set out in the formal agreement.

*Deliverables:* Agreed number of website and eBay updates per week or month (to be set in the formal agreement); clear list of what was updated; optional content calendar. Scope can be tailored to your catalogue size and how often new or used stock turns over.

### 3.2 Social Media Marketing (Optional)

For many small teams, social media is the first thing to drop. If you’d like a presence on **Facebook and Instagram** but don’t have time to post or promote, we can run it for you:

- **Light-touch social presence**  
  A consistent posting schedule (e.g. a set number of posts per week) with content that showcases your coffee machines, offers, and brand. We create and schedule; you approve as agreed.
- **Community management**  
  We monitor comments and messages and respond in line with your guidelines, escalating to you when needed—so you’re not tied to the phone or inbox.
- **Aligned with your paid search**  
  Messaging can be aligned with your Google Ads and website so your overall presence is consistent without you having to coordinate it.

*Deliverables:* Agreed number of posts per week per channel; optional community management; brief regular reports. This entire section can be added later if you prefer to start with website and eBay only.

### 3.3 Supporting and Optional Add-Ons

- **Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)**  
  On-page optimisation and keyword use so your website and product pages perform better in organic search—complementing your Google Ads.
- **AI Optimization (AIO)**  
  Where relevant, ensuring AI tools (e.g. ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews) find and represent Coffee Machine Warehouse accurately when people search for coffee machines or related services.
- **Reporting**  
  Clear, consolidated reports (e.g. weekly or monthly) on what we updated (website, eBay, and if applicable social), plus simple metrics so you can see the impact without extra admin.

  We need one main client contact, access to your website and eBay (or a listing feed you provide—to be agreed), and your brand guidelines. We propose a simple approval workflow (e.g. we submit updates in batches for sign-off; you confirm within an agreed timeframe) so you're not bottlenecked.
- **Website and eBay first**  
  We prioritise keeping your product and content accurate on the channels that drive sales. Social (if included) supports the same messaging without adding to your day.
- **Aligned with your Google Ads**  
  We do not manage or replace your paid search; we keep the website pages and product content it points to up to date so conversion improves.

  When a Team lead is in scope, they are your single point of contact; when not, you (or your nominated contact) supervise the team. Response times will be agreed in the formal agreement (e.g. 2 Business Days, Victoria Australia, for standard requests; 24 hours for critical/website outage).

  Services are delivered by our team from our Indian office (9 AM–6 PM IST) under the hours and SLAs set out in the formal agreement.
